2020 Topps Chrome Formula 1

2020 · Flagship · debut · Released April 2021 · 200-card base

The first-ever Topps Chrome Formula 1 product — the foundational set of the modern F1 card market, home to the George Russell, Lando Norris and Alexander Albon rookie cards and the SuperFractors that hold the sport's all-time price records (the Hamilton 1/1 famously sold for $900,000).

Hobby: 4 cards/pack, 18 packs/box, 12 boxes/case — 10 parallels or short prints and 9 inserts per box. Autographs ~1 per 2 boxes.

  • Lando Norris RC (#7)
  • Alexander Albon RC (#5)
  • Lewis Hamilton SuperFractor 1/1 — $900K record
  • Max Verstappen SuperFractor 1/1
