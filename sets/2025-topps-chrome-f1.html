◆ Topps Chrome · Formula 1

2025 Topps Chrome Formula 1

2025 · Flagship · Released January 2026 · 200-card base

The flagship F1 Chrome release for the 2025 season — a 200-card base set with a 30-plus parallel rainbow, F1's 75th-anniversary Diamond packs, and the first-ever Chrome on-card autograph (Lewis Hamilton).

Hobby: 8 cards/pack, plus one 75th Anniversary Diamond pack per box. Value blaster and LogoFractor editions released separately.

⭐ Key Cards

  • Kimi Antonelli RC (#8)
  • Lewis Hamilton Ferrari-era first Chrome on-card auto
  • Gabriel Bortoleto RC (#20)
  • Isack Hadjar & Liam Lawson RCs
