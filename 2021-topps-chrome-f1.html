2021 Topps Chrome Formula 1 Checklist

Year two — a 175-card base on the flagship design, introducing Checker Flag and RayWave parallels and the Hobby LITE format. Home to the debut F1 cards of Yuki Tsunoda, Mick Schumacher and Nikita Mazepin in the F1 Freshest subset.

Base set 175 cardsReleased March 9, 2021Brand Topps ChromeTier Flagship

Set structure

F1 and F2 subjects plus cars and crew; rookies sit in the F1 Freshest subset (#173–175).

F1 DriversF2 DriversCarsCrewF1 Freshest

Hobby (18 packs), Hobby LITE (RayWave exclusives, no autos), and Value Box SE. Autographs Hobby-only, not guaranteed.
