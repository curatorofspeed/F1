Reporting a data issue

If a board looks wrong — a price that seems off, a duplicate, a misattributed card — please include as much detail as you can:

  • The driver and the specific card (set, parallel, serial number if applicable).
  • A link to the sale or listing you're referencing.
  • What you believe the correct figure or detail should be.

This makes it much faster to verify and fix. See the Methodology page for how sales are sourced and cleaned in the first place.

F1 Card Index does not buy, sell, grade, or appraise cards, and does not provide individual investment advice. For valuation questions, the Price Guide and the live per-driver boards on the home page are the best starting points.
