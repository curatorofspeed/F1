About F1 Card Index

An independent, collector-built market tracker for the Formula 1 trading-card hobby.

F1 Card Index is a passion project built and maintained by a single Formula 1 fan and trading-card collector. It exists to do one thing well: make the modern F1 card market — led by Topps Chrome Formula 1 and the high-end Dynasty releases — legible to the people who actually collect it.

Why it exists

Formula 1 cards are a young hobby. The first licensed Topps Chrome F1 set landed in 2020, and the market has grown quickly and unevenly since. Unlike decades-old sports like basketball or baseball, there is no deep, public, decades-long pricing record to lean on. Comps are scattered across auction houses, marketplaces, and live-sale platforms, and they move fast around race weekends.

That gap is the reason this site exists. F1 Card Index pulls recent sold prices, live auctions, and record sales into one place, organized by driver, so a collector can answer the questions that actually matter: What has this card sold for? What is the ceiling for this driver? What is moving right now?

What you'll find here

  • Per-driver Top 10 sales boards — the highest verified sales we have tracked for each driver on the current grid.
  • Record cards — the single most valuable confirmed sale per driver.
  • A live market view — auctions ending soon and recent sold prices, refreshed continuously.
  • Driver power rankings — a quick read on which drivers' cards command the most, by top sale and by total tracked value.

How it's built

The site is intentionally lightweight and fast. Pricing data is aggregated from public marketplaces and auction results rather than stored as a proprietary database, and every board follows a consistent set of rules for what counts as a sale, how duplicates are handled, and how foreign-currency results are converted. You can read the full process on the Methodology page.

F1 Card Index is run independently and is not affiliated with Formula 1, the FIA, any team, Topps, or any marketplace. It's built by a collector, for collectors. If you spot an error in a board or have a sale you think should be tracked, the contact page is the fastest way to reach us.

Thanks for visiting — and enjoy the hunt.
