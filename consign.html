How you'll get paid

Payouts run on Stripe — nothing to set up now. When your card sells, we email you a private link where you connect your bank account directly with Stripe (takes about two minutes). Your money goes straight from Stripe to your bank; we never see or store your banking details.

🔒 Stripe handles payments for millions of businesses and is PCI Level 1 certified.
How it works: Listing is free — you set the price and keep the card. It lists as Buy It Now in the Index Shop, and buyers can also send you offers to accept or decline. When it sells, we collect and hold the payment and send you the buyer's shipping details — you ship the card directly to them with USPS tracking. Your payout is released as soon as tracking confirms delivery — your price minus our 7% fee. Holding funds until delivery protects both sides: buyers know their money is safe, and you're guaranteed payment once the card arrives. We never handle the card, and you're never charged up front.
