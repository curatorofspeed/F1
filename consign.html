How you'll get paid

Optional now — we confirm payout details by email before your card goes live. We only pay out after a sale.
How it works: Listing is free — you set the price and keep the card. It lists as Buy It Now in the Index Shop, and buyers can also send you offers to accept or decline. When it sells, we collect the payment and send you the buyer's shipping details — you ship the card directly to them with USPS. Once it's on the way, we pay you out: your price minus our 7% fee. We never handle or ship the card, and you're never charged up front — the fee only comes out of a completed sale.
